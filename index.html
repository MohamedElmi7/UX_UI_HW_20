UX Mobile Project:Â  Smol

Discover a new and easier way to support your local small businesses. With Smol, small business shoppers can find and follow their new favorite small businesses while also staying in the know about sales, hot drops, restocks, and more events hosted by small businesses in their area.

Project overview

The Problem: Many small business shoppers are not aware of the products and services offered by small businesses in their area. They lack the means to easily follow small businesses as well as to stay up to date with product availability across different small businesses. How might we enable small business shoppers to easily find and follow small businesses in the area, while giving them visibility to product availability, quality, source, and pricing information in order to encourage more small business shopping and support the local economy?

The Solution: Smol is an app that enables & facilitates users to discover and follow small businesses in their area, giving them access to product information such as availability, source, and pricing.

deliverables: User Research ( interviews/analysis), Definition & Synthesis, Brainstorming & Ideation, Prototyping, User Testing & Outcomes

Tools Used: Figma, Miro, Figjam, Google Suite, Trello

Team Size: 4 Members- Keauna Lyles, Nicole Tierney, Mohamed Elmi, Teddy Noun

Timeline & Scope: 3 weeks- (January 2023)
